Who am I?

Hello there!

I'm Victor Fateh a third year Computer Science student at San Jose State University.

Technology, photography and the automotive world's are my passions. I've been a semi-pro photographer and videographer for 3+ years, with major experience in automotive, landscape, and product work. I've done Automotive Event coverage, demo product imaging, and commercial video production.

Currently working at Shift Technologies, I love working in the automotive industry.

I live to learn. My CS experience covers: CSS, HTML5, C, Java, and Basic Android Development. Specifications on what I can do can be found here!
