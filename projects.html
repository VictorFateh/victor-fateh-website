Guess the Car1.0

Guess The Car is a carousel based quiz game where images of vehicles appear and the user must select the correct name. Image objects and their answers will be pulled randomly from a text file. Answers will be displayed and selected with radio buttons.

The initial build will have images hardcoded. Future plans will be to pull images and their answers from a designated car image page using scrapers.
